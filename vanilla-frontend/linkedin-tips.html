LinkedIn Masterclass

Make your LinkedIn attractive to recruiters using these precise AI-generated prompt tools.

Profile Photo Prompt

"Create a professional LinkedIn profile photo of a college student. Clean background (light grey or blue), formal attire, confident smile, natural lighting, sharp focus, corporate look, professional headshot style. No filters, no blur, realistic face, suitable for LinkedIn."

Tip: Wear a plain shirt • Face the camera • Avoid casual clothes

Banner / Cover Image Prompt

"Design a clean and professional LinkedIn banner for a student aspiring to work in the tech industry. Minimal design, modern colors, subtle icons related to technology, text space on the right, professional and corporate feel. No clutter, simple and readable."

Tip: Aspiring Software Engineer | Java | Open to Internships

About Section Prompt

"Write a professional LinkedIn “About” section for a college student. Include: Current education, Technical skills, Projects/internships, Career goal. Keep it between 150–250 words. Use a confident but simple tone."
